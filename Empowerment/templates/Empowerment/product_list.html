ABOUT US

We are a nonprofit organization who wants to empower women who don't have the oppurtunities that other individuals do around the world. Our mission is to direct this organization towards the Middle Eastern countries, becaue they are one of the main countries that don't allow for women to have basic rights through restrictions from both the law and customs imposed upon them. In the future, we hope to spread our organization impacts throughout the global society.

“To deny people their human rights is to challenge their very humanity.” – Nelson Mandela

To help these women, we created this website to promote some of the things that the Middle Eastern women may create with the little rights that they do have; such as baskets, rugs, jewelry, clothing, and much more. All proceeds made will go to these women who have made these things to achieve and obtain the most important thing that anyone can own: education.
